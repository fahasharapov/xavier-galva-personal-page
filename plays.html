THE PARLOUR by Xavier Galva.

Genre: comedy, dark comedy, drama, political (classism, Black Lives Matters, blue collar-working class, sexism)

We are thrown headlong into the hectic, hierarchical world of the New York City restaurant industry as Mitch, a new inexperienced manager takes a place at the top, followed by a tight-knit group of disarmingly hopeful and determined service employees. In this political pecking order, a privileged few may succeed, while the dignified suffer disempowerment. The Parlour is an ensemble comedy about the have and have-nots of urban existence, and the sacrifices we make for survival.
