Xavier Galva is a Dominican-American writer/performer/ teaching artist born in Washington Heights and raised in the Bronx. He trained as an actor at the Atlantic Acting School. He is a founding member of The Rattlestick's apprentice company: The Middle Voice. He’s a member of the 2014-2015 Clubbed Thumb Early Career Writers Group and Gingold Theatrical 2017 Speakers Corner Writers Group. His plays have been developed by MCC Theater, Clubbed Thumb, Rattlestick Playwriting Theater and The Middle Voice.

I was raised by an immigrant mother who sacrificed everything to give her four children a better life in the U.S. … I decided to pursue a life in the theater….
